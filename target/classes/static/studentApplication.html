Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I apply for a course?

A: Browse the "Available Courses" section, click on any course to view details, and click the "Apply" button.


Q: How can I track my application status?

A: Check the "My Applications" section and the Progress Tracker to see your current status.


Q: When will I hear back about my application?

A: You'll receive updates via email and can check your dashboard for real-time status changes.


For additional support, please contact our admissions team.
